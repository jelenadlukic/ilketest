About us

One Dot offers a comprehensive one-stop shop solution for foreign entities seeking to outsource their operations in Serbia. We enable our clients to focus entirely on the core, revenue-generating aspects of their business, while we manage the full spectrum of back-office functions, tailored to the needs of each client.

Our team and trusted partners provide expertise in incorporation, accounting, HR, recruitment, legal compliance, and work permits. By combining local know-how with international standards, we ensure that every company operates smoothly, legally, and efficiently in the Serbian market.

Whether you are opening a new subsidiary, hiring your first employees, or simply seeking reliable back-office support, One Dot is here to simplify the process. We are committed to building long-term partnerships, offering transparent pricing, and helping global businesses succeed in Serbia.
